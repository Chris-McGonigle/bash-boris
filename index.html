How to Play

Fed up with hiding in fridges, the UK Prime Minister Boris Johnson, has now taken to the sewers below Downing Street to evade his current nemesis….the Northern Ireland Protocol!

Take control as Ursula von der Leyen, and using the NI Protocol, ‘Bash Boris’ back into Number 10!!!

The rules are easy….Bash Boris as many times as you can within 30 seconds using your mouse or finger….be quick though….you never know where he will pop up next!

For the best user experience, we recommend using a desktop and mouse to enjoy this game.

If using a mobile or tablet device, orient your screen horizontally.
